Install on iPhone

🚨 You must use Safari. Chrome, Firefox, and other browsers on iPhone cannot install apps to the home screen. If you normally use Chrome, switch to Safari just for this step.
📱
iPhone / iPad — Safari
iOS 14 or later recommended
1
Open Safari and go to the URL
Type or paste https://waterboy2013.github.io/AutoKeeper/ into Safari's address bar and tap Go. The app should load and display the AutoKeeper interface — not a page of code.
2
Tap the Share button
Look at the bottom of the screen — tap the icon that looks like a box with an arrow pointing up. On iPad it may be in the top right.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the Share menu until you see "Add to Home Screen" — it has a plus icon. Tap it. You can rename it if you like, but "AutoKeeper" should already be filled in.
4
Tap "Add" in the top right
The AutoKeeper icon appears on your home screen immediately.
5
Always open from the home screen icon
Tap the AutoKeeper icon on your home screen — not the Safari bookmark. This opens it full-screen like a real app and keeps all your data in one place.
✓ After the first open, AutoKeeper works completely offline. No internet needed.
🤖
Android — Chrome

Common Questions

The URL just shows a README / text page — how do I get the app?
Make sure you're using the exact URL: https://waterboy2013.github.io/AutoKeeper/ with the slash at the end. If you're seeing the README, try adding index.html to the end of the URL as a temporary fix: https://waterboy2013.github.io/AutoKeeper/index.html
I don't see "Add to Home Screen" in Safari.
Make sure you're using Safari, not Chrome. In Safari, tap the Share button (box with arrow up) at the bottom of the screen. Scroll down in the menu — "Add to Home Screen" is further down the list, below the app shortcuts.
Will my data sync between my phone and computer?
No — data lives in each browser separately. To move data between devices, use the CSV export button inside the app, then use that as a backup record.
What happens if I clear my browser data?
Your AutoKeeper data will be erased. Export a CSV backup regularly, especially before clearing browser storage or switching phones.
Does it work without internet?
Yes — after you open it once, AutoKeeper is fully cached and works with no internet connection.
How do I get updates?
Updates are automatic. The next time you open the app while connected to the internet, it downloads the latest version in the background. You'll see the version number update in the header. Your data is never affected by updates.
I switched phones — how do I keep my data?
Before switching, use the CSV export button in the app to save your history. On the new phone, your logs won't transfer automatically (this app has no cloud sync), but you'll have your exported records as a reference. Re-add your vehicles manually on the new phone.

Email the developer directly — the address is included in your purchase receipt.
